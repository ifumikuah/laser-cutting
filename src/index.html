OUR SERVICES

2D Laser Cutting Service

2D laser cutting is a quick and easy method of manufacturing large quantities.

3D Laser Cutting Service

3D laser cutting is ideal for shaped blanks such as car parts.

CAD/CAM

CAD is the software we use to translate your design into a laser cut part.

2D Laser Scanning/Inspection & Reverse Engineering

Our 2D laser scanning and inspection service has two main roles: to ensure quality control and to reverse engineer a panel to a CAD file.

3D Laser Scanning/Inspection & Reverse Engineering

3D scanning and inspection is the best way to ensure the high quality our customers have come to expect.
